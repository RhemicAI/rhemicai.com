Cleaning is a volume-and-recurring business, and it's intensely competitive because switching costs for the customer are low. Winning more jobs comes down to being easy to find, fast to respond, and effortless to book, then turning one-time cleans into recurring revenue. Same system, with speed weighted especially heavily.

## Part 1: Get found

Homeowners and office managers find cleaners through Google, the map pack, and increasingly by asking AI for a recommendation. Cover all three:

- **A complete, active Google Business Profile** with categories (residential, commercial, deep clean, move-out), hours, photos, and recent reviews.
- **Service pages** for each service, structured with LocalBusiness schema, answering pricing approach, what's included, and scheduling. The how-to is in [how to show up in ChatGPT for your trade](/blog/how-to-show-up-in-chatgpt-for-your-trade).
- **Reviews**, which carry enormous weight in cleaning because trust (people in your home or office) is the whole decision.

## Part 2: Answer fast, because speed wins cleaning

Cleaning customers shop. They message several companies and book the first that responds with a clear quote and easy scheduling. This makes speed-to-lead the single biggest lever in the trade, more than in almost any other. A missed call or a next-day reply is a lost contract, and a contract is recurring revenue worth far more than one clean.

The capture system:

- **Answer every call and message** fast, with a system, not whenever someone is free. See [the capture system](/blog/stop-losing-leads-to-missed-calls).
- **Recover misses instantly** with a text back, the channel cleaning customers prefer.
- **Respond in minutes and make booking effortless.** The deeper case for this is in [speed to lead for home services](/blog/speed-to-lead-for-home-services).

## Part 3: Convert to recurring and track what works

The real value in cleaning is recurring contracts. Capture the first job fast, deliver, and convert to a schedule. Then track which sources produce recurring customers, not just one-time cleans, using [closed-loop attribution for home services](/blog/closed-loop-attribution-for-home-services).

## The bottom line

Cleaning jobs go to the company that's easy to find, fastest to respond, and effortless to book, then converts to recurring. Speed is your edge. Build the system around it.
