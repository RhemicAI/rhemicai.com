For a home service contractor, the Google Business Profile is the single highest-leverage piece of local visibility. It drives the map pack, which often outranks the blue links for "near me" searches, and it feeds the AI recommendations homeowners increasingly rely on. Get it right and you're found and chosen. Neglect it and you're invisible where local demand actually looks.

## Why it matters more than your website for local

For local intent, the map pack sits above the organic links and gets the clicks and calls. That pack is powered almost entirely by your Google Business Profile. And the same profile data feeds AI assistants and AI Overviews when they recommend a contractor. So one well-maintained profile improves your map ranking and your AI visibility at the same time. That's leverage you don't get anywhere else.

## The optimization checklist

**Complete every field.** Google's own guidance is explicit: complete and accurate information makes you more likely to show in local results. Don't leave fields blank.

**Get categories right.** Choose the most accurate primary category, then add relevant secondary categories. This is one of the strongest relevance signals for which searches you appear in.

**List services and service areas.** Spell out what you do and where you do it. This helps Google and AI match you to the right queries.

**Add real photos.** Actual work, team, and trucks, not stock. Recent photos signal an active business.

**Keep hours accurate**, including holiday hours. Wrong hours kill trust and send callers elsewhere.

**Build a review engine.** Volume, recency, and sentiment are among the strongest local signals. Ask every happy customer, make it easy, and keep it steady. A flow of recent reviews beats an old high rating.

**Respond to reviews**, good and bad. It signals an active, accountable business and is a trust signal both Google and homeowners weigh.

## Keep it active, not set-and-forget

A profile that's filled out once and abandoned decays in competitiveness. Post updates, add photos, keep reviews flowing, and update services seasonally. Active profiles outperform dormant ones, and AI engines weight recency.
